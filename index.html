About us

Mumbai Public School is a school which gives quality education to each and every student. Mumbai Public School is located in Powai, Mumbai, Maharashtra. Mumbai Public School is open for all the students from class 1 to 12. the aim of Mumbai Public School is to provide quality education at a minimal cost .The school aims at nurturing the innate potential of the students and mould them into passionate, committed, competent, sensitive, value based individuals with leadership qualities. The school is a co-educational English medium Senior Secondary school recognized by the Directorate of Education and affiliated to Central Board of Secondary Education under All India 10+2 pattern in both science and commerce streams.MPS set up in 2008 with the objective of providing quality, affordable and meaningful education to the children and teenager.The school has been navigating itself in the directing of its goals producing excellent Board results year after, thereby, living up to the exception of the stakeholder and making a mark for itself in this short span of its existence.
